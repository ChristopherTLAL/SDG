### 4.3 Financial Maintenance Requirement (Visa)

For visa application, students must demonstrate:
- **London:** £1,529/month × 9 months = £13,761
- **Outside London:** £1,171/month × 9 months = £10,539

This is a proof-of-funds requirement, not an additional cost.

## Notes & Caveats

1. **Fee variability:** Many universities (especially UCL, Manchester, KCL, Southampton, Birmingham) publish fees per-programme rather than in neat bands. The ranges given are estimates based on available data.

2. **Year-on-year inflation:** Most universities increase international fees by 2-5% annually. The figures here are for 2025-26 entry; 2026-27 will be higher.

3. **Fixed vs. variable fees:** Some universities (UCL, Sheffield, Glasgow) fix fees at the point of entry for the duration of your programme. Others (Southampton from 2026, most English universities) increase fees each year.

4. **Scottish 4-year degrees:** Edinburgh, Glasgow, and St Andrews run 4-year undergraduate programmes (vs. 3 years in England), which increases total cost by one additional year of tuition + living.

5. **Cambridge College fees:** Cambridge charges both University tuition and College fees. The College fee (~£11,000–£12,500/yr) is a significant additional cost not charged at other universities.

6. **Oxford College fees:** Oxford does NOT charge separate College fees for most programmes (these are included in the tuition fee). However, some programmes may have additional costs.

7. **LSE and Imperial specialisms:** LSE offers only social sciences/business/law (no natural sciences or engineering). Imperial offers only STEM and business (no humanities/social sciences).

8. **Exchange rates:** All figures in GBP. For CNY conversion, use current exchange rate (~1 GBP = 9.2–9.5 CNY as of early 2026).
